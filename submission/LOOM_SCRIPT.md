# NIPUN Compass — 60-Second Loom Walkthrough Script

**Target:** 60 seconds. One take. Screen share: app open on Bihar, Priority Queue loaded.
**Tone:** DEO-first. Every sentence names a person, a pain, or a number.

---

## [0–10s] The Monday Problem

> "It's Monday morning. You're a District Education Officer in Bihar. You oversee 847 government schools. Your DM wants to know what you're doing about Grade 3 literacy — and you have a 200-page ASER PDF that tells you the district average dropped 4 points. It does not tell you which schools are driving that drop. Until now."

*[Screen: app landing page — Priority Queue already loaded for Bihar, Muzaffarpur district]*

---

## [10–30s] Priority Queue → Click a School → Diagnosis

> "NIPUN Compass opens on your priority queue. Right now it's telling me: these are the 20 schools in Muzaffarpur that need a BEO visit this week — ranked by reading gap, math gap, years of decline, and months since the last visit. I can see exactly why each school is ranked where it is. I copy this list to WhatsApp in one click."

*[Click "Copy this week's list to WhatsApp" expander — show the text block]*

> "Now I click into the top school — one click — and I get its diagnosis."

*[Click "→ Diagnose" button on card #1]*

> "Grade 3 reading is 22 points below the district average. It's been declining three years straight. No intervention logged in 14 months. The paragraph writes itself."

*[Show the diagnosis paragraph and trend chart]*

---

## [30–45s] Interventions + Tracker

> "Now the DEO asks: what do I actually do? I hit 'Get interventions' and the system retrieves three evidence-backed programs matched to this school's specific gap — from NIPUN Bharat and Pratham's TaRL framework. Each one shows what it costs, how long it takes, and where the evidence comes from."

*[Navigate to Interventions page, show 3 results]*

> "I launch one. It's now logged in the tracker — school name, intervention, start date, target metric. In 3 months, the BEO submits a micro-test score and I see a before/after bar chart."

*[Navigate to Tracker, show an existing logged intervention with the before/after chart]*

---

## [45–60s] Monthly Review + Close

> "Last thing — my 3 PM DM meeting. I click Monthly Review. One page: district summary, block breakdown, top 5 interventions and their outcomes, trajectory vs ASER baseline. I export to PDF. Done."

*[Navigate to Monthly Review, show the district summary, click Export PDF]*

> "That's NIPUN Compass. Not a dashboard — a tool a DEO actually opens on Monday morning. Link in the description."

*[End on the app — Priority Queue visible]*

---

**Notes for recording:**
- Record at 1280×720, browser zoom at 90%
- Pre-load Bihar / Muzaffarpur so there's no spinner on camera
- Keep mouse movements deliberate — evaluator is reading + watching simultaneously
- Do not narrate what you're clicking — narrate what the DEO is *deciding*
