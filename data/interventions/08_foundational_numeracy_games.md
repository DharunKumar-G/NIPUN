# Foundational Numeracy Games — Physical, Low-Tech

**Program:** Foundational Numeracy Games (NIPUN Bharat FLN Kit — Math Component)
**Source:** NCERT FLN Activity Kit (Math), 2021; Pratham Math TaRL activity documentation; ASER 2022 math findings
**Target grades:** Grade 3–5 (subtraction and division gaps)
**Subject:** Math
**Estimated cost:** ₹150–300 per classroom (one-time materials; durable for 2+ years)
**Typical duration:** 30 minutes daily during the FLN block; no separate camp needed

---

## What It Does

ASER 2022 found that only 25.6% of Grade 3 children in Bihar could do 2-digit subtraction, and only 8.7% could divide. These gaps are not about intelligence — they reflect that children never encountered concrete representations of place value and grouping. Low-tech numeracy games address this directly: number-line floor mats (drawn with chalk), place-value blocks made from bundled popsicle sticks, card games for number recognition (matching numeral to quantity), and "market games" where children buy and sell items using play currency. All materials are either free (chalk, sticks) or cost ₹150–300 to print and laminate per classroom.

## Evidence

NCERT's FLN Activity Kit math evaluation (2022, 8 pilot districts across 4 states) found classrooms using the activity-based math kit daily showed a 16 percentage point increase in Grade 3 subtraction correct-response rates over 20 weeks, versus 5 percentage points in textbook-only classrooms. Pratham's TaRL math documentation (Duflo, Banerjee et al., J-PAL) shows hands-on grouping activities are the single highest-impact individual technique for bridging the "number recognition → subtraction" gap. Materials are freely downloadable from the DIKSHA platform.

## What the DEO Does

Order NCERT FLN math kits from DIET for the 10 priority schools with worst Grade 3 or Grade 5 math scores. Each kit has a 20-page facilitator guide. BEO demonstrates kit use at a 1-hour teacher orientation during the next school visit. No extra time in the school day required — kit activities replace textbook exercises during the existing 30-minute math period.

## Success Metrics

- Kits delivered and teacher orientation complete within 3 weeks
- BEO observes kit in active use on next school visit
- Mini-test at 3 months: target 40% of Grade 3 children reach "subtraction" level from "number recognition"
