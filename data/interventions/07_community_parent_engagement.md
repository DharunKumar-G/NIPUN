# Community and Parent Engagement Programs

**Program:** School Management Committee (SMC) Activation and Parent Reading Programs
**Source:** RTE Act 2009 (SMC mandate); Pratham "Read India" campaign documentation; ASER 2022 community findings
**Target grades:** Grade 1–5 (home literacy environment affects all grades)
**Subject:** Reading (home practice component)
**Estimated cost:** ₹100–200 per school (printing + one community meeting facilitation)
**Typical duration:** Ongoing; quarterly SMC meetings + monthly parent reading sessions

---

## What It Does

ASER surveys consistently find that children who are read to at home — or who have someone at home who can read with them — perform 10–15 percentage points higher on Grade 3 oral reading tasks, independent of school quality. Community engagement programs have two components: (1) SMC activation — the legally mandated School Management Committee is required under RTE 2009 to meet monthly; many never do. An active SMC creates accountability for teacher attendance and learning outcomes. (2) Parent reading sessions — parents (including those who are themselves barely literate) are taught a single technique: 15-minute oral story-telling using NCERT picture books, which the school lends to families weekly.

## Evidence

Pratham's "Read India" campaign (2007–2012, documented in annual reports) found that villages with active community reading volunteers showed Grade 3 reading gains of 8–12 percentage points over 2 years versus comparison villages. ASER 2022 notes that districts in Himachal Pradesh with high SMC meeting frequency also show consistently higher Grade 3 reading scores — one of the factors explaining HP's top-quartile performance. Note: correlation, not controlled experiment; other state-level factors may confound.

## What the DEO Does

Identify 5 schools where Grade 3 reading is low AND teacher attendance is also low (often correlated). Direct BEO to call an SMC meeting within 2 weeks — BEO facilitates, parents and village panchayat members attend. At the meeting, distribute 10 NCERT picture books per school (from DIET stock) for home lending. Record SMC meeting minutes in the school file.

## Success Metrics

- SMC meetings held in all 5 target schools within 3 weeks
- Book lending register active at 3-month BEO visit
- 6-month Grade 3 reading mini-test in target schools
