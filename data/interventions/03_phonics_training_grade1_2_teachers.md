# Phonics Training for Grade 1–2 Teachers

**Program:** Structured Phonics Pedagogy Training (NIPUN Bharat teacher capacity module)
**Source:** NCERT FLN Training Module 2 (2021); DIET-level implementation guidelines
**Target grades:** Grade 1–2 teachers (affects Grade 3 outcomes within 1 year)
**Subject:** Reading
**Estimated cost:** ₹1,200–1,800 per teacher for 3-day residential; ₹400 for in-service day model
**Typical duration:** 3-day residential training; monthly follow-up by BEO (1 school visit)

---

## What It Does

Grade 1–2 teachers in Hindi-belt states often teach reading by having children memorize whole words (sight-reading) rather than decoding letter-sound relationships. This makes Grade 3 reading data look catastrophic — children can recognize familiar words but cannot read any new word. Phonics training teaches teachers the NIPUN-aligned structured literacy sequence: consonant-vowel mapping, blending, segmenting, and decodable text reading. Training uses NCERT's FLN print kit and the DIKSHA platform for refresher videos.

## Evidence

NCERT's Foundational Literacy and Numeracy (FLN) framework (2021) cites international meta-analyses showing structured phonics instruction raises early reading outcomes by 0.6–0.8 standard deviations over whole-language methods. In Rajasthan's Pehli Kiran program (2020–21), DIETs that ran 3-day phonics workshops showed measurably higher Grade 2 oral reading fluency at year-end compared to untrained blocks. ASER 2022 notes that states with structured FLN teacher training showed faster Grade 3 reading recovery post-COVID.

## What the DEO Does

Nominate the 3 lowest-performing blocks. Coordinate with DIET principal to schedule a 3-day training at block resource centre. Arrange BEO follow-up school visit at 30 days and 90 days to observe classroom practice. No new budget needed — use existing NIPUN Bharat teacher training allocation.

## Success Metrics

- All nominated Grade 1–2 teachers complete training within 4 weeks
- BEO classroom observation checklist: teacher using phonics sequence in lesson
- Grade 3 reading scores at 6-month mini-test compared to baseline
