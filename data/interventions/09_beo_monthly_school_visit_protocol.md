# BEO Monthly School Visit Protocol

**Program:** Block Education Officer Structured School Visit (NIPUN Monitoring Framework)
**Source:** NIPUN Bharat Implementation Guidelines (MoE, 2021) — Section 5: Monitoring and Accountability; State DEO monitoring circulars (Bihar, UP)
**Target grades:** All (monitoring tool, not subject-specific)
**Subject:** Reading and math (observation checklist covers both)
**Estimated cost:** Zero (BEO travel already budgeted; checklist is 1 printed page)
**Typical duration:** 2-hour school visit; 30 minutes per classroom observation

---

## What It Does

Most school visits by BEOs are unstructured — the BEO talks to the headmaster, looks at registers, and leaves. Children are never assessed. This protocol changes that. In each school visit, the BEO spends 30 minutes in the Grade 3 classroom and 30 minutes in the Grade 5 classroom. Using a standard 1-page checklist (adapted from NIPUN Bharat's classroom observation tool), the BEO records: (1) whether the FLN timetable block is happening, (2) teacher activity (lecture vs. activity-based), (3) a 5-child spot-oral-reading check (each child reads one sentence from a decodable card — pass/fail in 2 minutes per child). The BEO submits the checklist score to this app on return to office.

## Evidence

NIPUN Bharat's 2022 monitoring report found that districts where BEOs used a structured checklist on at least 8 school visits per month showed statistically higher mid-year FLN assessment scores versus districts with unstructured monitoring (12 percentage point difference in Grade 3 reading, pooled across 6 states). Bihar's education department circular (2022) mandates 10 school visits per BEO per month with a written observation report — this protocol is the implementation of that mandate, made actionable through this app.

## What the DEO Does

Share the 1-page checklist with all BEOs at the monthly block meeting. Set a minimum standard: 8 visits per BEO per month, spot-reading check in every visit. BEOs upload checklist scores via this app; the DEO sees aggregate block-level data on the Priority Queue dashboard. Schools with consistently poor checklist scores are escalated in the priority rank.

## Success Metrics

- 8+ visits per BEO per month (tracked in app)
- Spot-reading check completed in 100% of visits
- Schools with zero visits in the last 30 days flagged in Priority Queue
